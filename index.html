Welcome to Laundry Wallah / Laundry Mart

Laundry Wallah / Laundry Mart is a modern and innovative solution to the everyday chore of laundry. Born from the need for convenient, high-quality, and affordable laundry services, it operates on a franchise model, establishing a network of tech-enabled laundry stores across various neighborhoods. These stores offer a comprehensive range of services, including washing, drying, folding, and ironing, all managed through a user-friendly digital platform. By leveraging technology to streamline operations and provide seamless customer experience, Laundry Wallah aims to become the go-to destination for all laundry needs, transforming a mundane task into a hassle-free and professional service. Its scalable and efficient business model makes it an attractive venture for entrepreneurs looking to enter the burgeoning service industry.

Our Services

  • Wash & Fold: This is our most popular service. We use high-quality detergents and softeners to wash your clothes, then professionally fold them, ready to be put away.
  • Wash & Iron: Get perfectly pressed clothes without the effort. We wash and dry your garments and then expertly iron each piece, so they're ready to wear.
  • Dry Cleaning: For delicate fabrics and special garments that require professional care, our dry cleaning service ensures your clothes are cleaned safely and effectively, maintaining their texture and color
  • Express Service: Need your laundry back in a hurry? Our express service provides a quick turnaround so you can get your clothes cleaned and ready in no time.
  • Specialized Cleaning: We also handle specific items like blankets, curtains, and quilts, giving them the thorough cleaning they need.

Key Features

  1. Tech-Enabled: Order and track services via our app.
  2. Franchise Model: A network of reliable, local stores.
  3. Comprehensive Services: Wash & Fold, Wash & Iron, Dry Cleaning, and more.
  4. Quality Assurance: Professional-grade equipment and eco-friendly detergents.
